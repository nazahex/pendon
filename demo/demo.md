## 1. Text Emphasis

You can easily change the writing style of your text:

- _Italicized text_ using asterisks or underscores.
- **Bold text** using two asterisks or two underscores.
- _**Bold and italicized text**_ using three of those symbols.
- ~~Strikethrough text~~ using two tildes.
